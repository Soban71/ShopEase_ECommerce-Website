Help Center - Frequently Asked Questions

🛍️ What is the purpose of this e-commerce app?
This app helps users browse, purchase, and manage products easily with features like cart, wishlist, order tracking, and customer support.
📦 How can I track my order?
After placing an order, go to the "My Orders" section and click "Track Order" next to your order.
💳 What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, UPI, and cash on delivery (where available).
🔄 Can I return a product after purchase?
Yes, you can return products within 7 days of delivery from the "My Orders" section.
🔒 Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and data privacy protocols to protect your personal information.
📞 How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us through live chat, email at support@example.com, or by calling +1 800 123 4567.
🛒 Can I save products to buy later?
Yes, use the "Add to Wishlist" feature to save products for future purchases.
🕒 What are the delivery times?
Most orders are delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your location and selected shipping method.
🧾 How can I download an invoice?
Go to "My Orders", select your order, and click on "Download Invoice".
🎁 Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes, gift wrapping is available at checkout for a small additional charge.
✏️ Can I edit my shipping address after placing an order?
Yes, you can edit the shipping address within 1 hour of placing the order. Go to "My Orders" and click "Edit Address".
🧍 Do I need to create an account to place an order?
While guest checkout is available, creating an account allows for order tracking, faster checkout, and access to offers.
💡 How do I apply a promo code or discount?
Enter your promo code in the "Apply Promo" field during checkout and click "Apply" to see the updated total.
🚚 Can I choose the delivery speed?
Yes, you can choose Standard, Express, or Next-Day delivery options at checkout depending on your location.
🔁 What if I receive a defective or wrong item?
Please contact support immediately through "My Orders" > "Report Issue". We’ll arrange a replacement or refund.
